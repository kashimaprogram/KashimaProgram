Flow

① Prior orientation

Invited people from Kashima City Hall, a Kashima City community development NPO, and a Kashima tourism company to learn about the current situation and tourism issues in Kashima City.

② Training

Students spent 1 night and 2 days visiting the main tourist attractions in Kashima City to gather materials for video and poster production.

③ Post training

Students gathered in groups and worked on their poster and video.

④ Results presentation

Each group presented their completed works. The judging was attended by people from Kashima City.

⑤ Presentation at Kashima city hall

Video and poster presentations were made at Kashima City Hall.
