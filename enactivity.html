Flow

① Prior orientation

Invited people from Kashima City Hall, a local development NPO in Kashima City, and a tourism company in Kashima to learn about the current situation of Kashima City and the challenges of tourism.

③ Post training

The students gathered in groups and worked on their poster and video.

⑤ Presentation at Kashima city hall

Made videos and posters presentation at Kashima city hall.

② Training

Over two days and one night, students visited major sightseeing spots in Kashima City and collected materials for video and poster production.

④ Results presentation

Each group presented their completed work. The judging was attended by people from Kashima City.
