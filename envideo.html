Video

Select a video to play it. Subtitle is available.

Kashima can be toured quickly in a day. However, you can experience the charm of Kashima more by staying longer and having various experiences such as making pickles and tasting sake. We made this video with the hope that you will stay in Kashima and enjoy the island. We hope you will find it useful as a model course.

The theme of the video is "Introducing a model course for a trip to Kashima." We chose this theme because we were able to fully enjoy Kashima during our actual 2 day 1 night training program and wanted to convey the attractiveness of the city as it is. The video introduces many of the sights of Kashima, and we hope it will be helpful to you on your trip.

There are two key points to our video. The first is that we want people from overseas to watch our videos, not only in Japanese. We wanted people to know more about Kashima, so we added subtitles in English as well as Japanese. The concept of this program is "connect to inbound tourism," and we created the video in two languages so that people from all walks of life can become aware of the beauty of Kashima. Second, we also incorporated the sounds of the surrounding area. We tried to make it possible to imagine what the place actually looks like through the sounds, so that the viewer can imagine the scenery and feel more inclined to visit Kashima.

We created this video with a story woven into it in the hope that people will feel the attractiveness of Kashima that can be found by actually walking around the city. It expresses the attractiveness of Kashima as we felt it. We hope that through this work, people will be inspired to create their own unique and wonderful trip with Kashima.
