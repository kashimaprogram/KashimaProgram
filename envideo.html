Video

Play a video by selecting it. Subtitle is available.

It would be possible to quickly tour Kashima in one day. However, if you stay longer and experience various things such as making pickles and tasting sake, you will feel the charm of Kashima more. We made this video with the hope that people will stay in Kashima and enjoy the city at their leisure. We hope you will find this video helpful as it follows a model course.

The theme is "Introducing a model course for a trip to Kashima". Since we were able to enjoy Kashima to the fullest during the two days and one night of training we actually did, we chose this theme because we wanted to convey the charm of Kashima as it is. In the video, we introduce many sightseeing spots in Kashima, which we hope will be helpful for your trip.

There are two main points to our video. The first is that we want people from overseas to watch our videos, not only in Japanese, but also in English, so that they can learn more about Kashima. The second was to incorporate the sounds of the surrounding environment so that people can imagine what the place is really like through the sounds, and to increase their desire to visit Kashima.

We created this video with a story in mind, hoping that people would feel the charm of Kashima that can be found by actually walking around the city. It expresses the appeal of Kashima as we perceive it. We hope that through this work, you will want to create your own wonderful trip with Kashima.
