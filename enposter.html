The photos are taken from various angles and distances to give you a sense of Kashima's nature and culture. The photos show a wide variety of sights, such as food, living things, buildings, decorations, and the tidal flat that stretches out in front of us.

The poster has three particulars.The first is the design of the poster. The first is the design of the poster, which is retro and nostalgic. The second was to put a lot of pictures on the poster. The second was to put a lot of photos on the poster. The third was to include cherry blossoms, the flower of Kashima City, in the poster.

The first thing to look at was the concept that the cover of the poster should have an impact. For the front cover, we used a photo of a tidal flat as the background. The back of the poster shows the typical tourist spots in Kashima, as well as spots that people don't usually notice, from the perspective of a student.

In order to express the time spent in Kashima City on the cover, the design was inspired by nostalgic memories that seem to have faded a bit. The back of the booklet is divided into three categories: "seeing", "experiencing" and "staying" so that you can get a rough idea of what you can do in Kashima with just one glance.
