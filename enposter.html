Poster

Select an image to enlarge it.

We have arranged photos taken from various angles and distances to give visitors a sense of the nature and culture of Kashima City. By placing a wide variety of attractions such as food, creatures, buildings, decorations, and the mudflats that stretch out before eyes, we convey the charm of Kashima.

The posters have 3 particular features. The first is the design of the poster. Second, we included many photos on the poster. The third was to include cherry blossoms, the city flower of Kashima in the poster.

The first highlight of the poster was the concept that, since it was a poster, the cover had to have a strong impact. The front cover features a photo of a tidal flat in the background. The back of the poster features typical tourist attractions of Kashima, as well as spots that you would not normally notice from a student's point of view.

To express the time spent in Kashima City on the cover, the design was inspired by nostalgic memories that seem to have faded a bit. On the reverse side, we divided the tourist attractions in Kashima into three categories: "to see," "to experience," and "to stay," so that visitors can get a general idea of what they can do at a given sightseeing spot just by looking at it.
